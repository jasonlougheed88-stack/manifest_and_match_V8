## KEY FINDINGS

### Market Trends
- **Cloud-based solutions** dominate across all sectors (87% of platforms)
- **AI integration** becoming standard in 2024-2025
- **All-in-one platforms** replacing multiple separate tools
- **Mobile-first** approach critical for field-based industries
- **API integrations** essential for modern workflows

### Industry-Specific Insights

**Healthcare:** Epic and Oracle Cerner control 50%+ of hospital market; smaller practices favor athenahealth and AdvancedMD

**Finance:** Oracle leads with 6.5% market share; highly fragmented market with hundreds of specialized tools

**Legal:** Clio dominates small-to-medium firms; enterprise favors iManage and NetDocuments

**Education:** Canvas, Blackboard, Moodle, and D2L Brightspace serve 91% of student enrollments

**Manufacturing:** SAP, Oracle, and Microsoft Dynamics dominate ERP; Autodesk leads CAD market

**Construction:** Procore market leader; BIM adoption accelerating (projected $21B market by 2032)

**Hospitality:** Toast POS #1 for restaurants; 85% prioritize PMS integration for hotels

**Retail:** Shopify dominates omnichannel; Square for small businesses

**Transportation & Logistics:** Market growing to $57B by 2035; AI route optimization standard

**Marketing & Sales:** Salesforce holds largest CRM market share; HubSpot leads SMB segment

**Human Resources:** Workday, SAP, and Oracle control enterprise; BambooHR leads SMB

**Real Estate:** 535+ MLS systems in US; CRM integration critical (NAR 2024 survey)

**Creative & Media:** Adobe dominates but Affinity, DaVinci, and Canva gaining market share

**Non-Profit:** Unified platforms replacing separate donor/volunteer systems; market consolidating
